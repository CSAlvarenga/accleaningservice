Our Services

we provide different types of service

House Cleaning

A typical cleaning service can include dusting and vacuuming floors, wiping down surfaces in the bathroom and kitchen, emptying trash cans, scrubbing sinks and toilets, washing dishes and doing laundry (double check during booking), and cleaning out difficult to reach areas like under beds.

Office Cleaning

This keeps office spaces clean and tidy to help boost productivity in the workplace. Medical facility cleaning – this type of cleaning service is very important as medical facilities are high traffic areas that are exposed to a number of germs and bacteria

Construction Cleaning

Remove all trash from the property, including lumber and debris. Removing protective film from windows and glass (Goop) Washing exterior windows and doors. Sweeping and dusting any entry doorways. Sweeping and/or power washing the driveway and any other paved surfaces.
